Our Services

At EDUTECH, we offer a range of services to enhance the educational experience and facilitate efficient learning and teaching. Our services include:

1. Question Paper Generation

We provide a robust question paper generation feature that allows teachers to create custom assessments. Our software randomly selects questions from a database of previous year question papers, enabling teachers to generate unique and diverse question papers quickly and effortlessly.

2. Frequently Asked Questions Compilation

EDUTECH identifies frequently asked questions from the input papers and compiles them into a comprehensive resource for students. This compilation assists students in focusing their studies on the most important and commonly recurring topics, aiding their exam preparation.

3. Question Paper Contribution

We invite authenticated individuals, such as experienced educators and subject matter experts, to contribute new question papers to our database. This collaborative effort ensures the continuous growth of our resources and fosters an environment of shared knowledge.

4. Easy-to-Use Interface

Our user-friendly interface is designed to make navigation and usage intuitive for both teachers and students. With a clean and organized layout, users can quickly access the desired features and seamlessly navigate through the software.
